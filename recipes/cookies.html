Beyond Soft Cookies

Do you love cookies that are so soft that Grandma can eat them without her dentures. Well, these are so soft, they begin to melt as they approach your lips. Show Grandma some love and bake these cookies today.

Ingredients

  • 21\4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1\4 cup sugar
  • 3\4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 pkg (4 oz. serving) instant vanilla pudding
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pkg (12 oz) chocolate chips
  • 1 cup nuts (optional)
  • Back to Top

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375° Fahrenheit — (190° Celcius).
  2. Combine butter, the sugars, vanilla and pudding mix in large bowl.
  3. Beat until creamy. Then beat in eggs.
  4. Gradually add flour, soda, chips and nuts. Batter will be stiff.
  5. Scoop one tablespoon of batter and roll it into a ball, then place them about 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until the edge is slightly brown. Try not to over cook.
